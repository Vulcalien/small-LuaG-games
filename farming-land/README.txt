This LuaG Console game was made for the Fantasy Console Game Jam 4 in
July 2019.

It is a very simple farming game, where the player endlessly buys seeds,
plants them and harvests the wheat.

Press 'A' on top of the 'BUY SEEDS' area to buy seeds. Press 'A' to
plant seeds and to harvest the wheat. Reach 1000 coins to win.
